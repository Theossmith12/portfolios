Green Exchange: A Climate & Environment Project

This project was developed as part of the University of Exeter's Grand Challenges, focusing on Climate & Environment. The Green Exchange is a community-driven space where students can exchange items, promoting sustainability and reducing waste. Additionally, it serves as a venue for speeches and presentations, fostering collaboration among students and lecturers.
